💻 Quick Start

Basic Playlist

<div data-waveform-playlist>
  <div data-track data-url="song1.mp3" data-title="Track 1"></div>
  <div data-track data-url="song2.mp3" data-title="Track 2"></div>
</div>

Podcast with Chapters

<div data-waveform-playlist>
  <div data-track data-url="episode.mp3" data-title="Episode 1">
    <div data-chapter data-time="0:00">Introduction</div>
    <div data-chapter data-time="5:30">Main Topic</div>
    <div data-chapter data-time="45:00">Conclusion</div>
  </div>
</div>

With All Options

<div data-waveform-playlist
     data-waveform-style="mirror"
     data-continuous="true"
     data-show-play-state="true">
  <div data-track
       data-url="audio.mp3"
       data-title="Title"
       data-subtitle="Artist"
       data-artwork="cover.jpg">
  </div>
</div>
